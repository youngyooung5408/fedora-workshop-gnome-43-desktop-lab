## Completed design

- Replace the market header plus control with a pen. Editing reveals an inline × beside every instrument and a separate plus control; leaving edit mode hides both destructive controls and closes the chooser.
- Keep the chooser compact: search after a short typing pause, constrain up to 24 verified results to a fixed scrolling area, remove the standalone search button and selected-instrument description list, and collapse optional international key entry behind a key icon.
- Preserve the live first-workspace widget actor and create only a disposable, non-interactive `Clutter.Clone` inside GNOME Shell 49's active overview workspace background. The replica zooms with the wallpaper and is destroyed safely without reparenting the live actor.
- Remove the custom Show Applications action and suppress GNOME's bottom overview dash while the extension is enabled, restoring its exact prior state on disable.
- Listen to `Shell.AppSystem` application-state changes and append unique running applications to a bounded scrolling section at the bottom of the custom left dock.
- Preserve the accepted marine styling, existing fullscreen/maximized/left-tiled suppression, dock editor, provider validation and caching, Bluetooth behavior, Codex usage indicator, GNOME settings, and host-safe feature ownership.
