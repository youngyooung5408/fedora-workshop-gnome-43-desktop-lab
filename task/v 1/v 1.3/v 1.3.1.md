## Completed design

- Preserve the accepted v1.2.14 marine presentation while replacing the dock editor with a selectable group list, selected-group details, common symbolic icons, installed-app search, and an ordered current-app list.
- Save group creation, rename, icon, order, deletion, and app changes immediately; refuse the final-group deletion and duplicate app entries.
- Store market selections as provider, symbol, official name, exchange, and currency records. Read legacy strings and migrate them only after a provider resolves them successfully.
- Search and quote Taiwan securities from the official keyless TWSE and TPEx OpenAPI closing datasets. Use Twelve Data for supported international instruments and store its user key only in `~/.config/desktop-lab-v12/twelve-data-key` with mode `0600`.
- Cache the last successful price and keep it visible during temporary network or provider failures.
- Keep clock and market inside one stable first-workspace background actor. Fade it for Activities/app search, never reparent it into private overview actors, stop its clock repaint while hidden, and guard timers and asynchronous callbacks across disable/destruction.
- Enumerate every connected BlueZ device even without `Battery1`, merge matching UPower data, infer mouse, keyboard, headset, or generic icons from metadata, and never synthesize an unavailable percentage.
- Preserve the reorganized `task/v 1/` hierarchy, register changed feature revisions explicitly, carry forward unchanged feature revisions, and keep recovery-advisor files out of the release.
