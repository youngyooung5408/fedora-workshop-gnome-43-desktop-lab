## Completed design

- Keep the accepted v1.3.3 placement in GNOME Shell's active main-workspace full-monitor wallpaper group and keep the live desktop clock/market actor stable.
- Put the disposable `Clutter.Clone` inside its own allocation frame instead of allowing `Clutter.BinLayout` to stretch the clone directly across the wallpaper group.
- Keep the clone at the live layer's native monitor dimensions and apply one shared `Math.min(width ratio, height ratio)` transform to both `scale_x` and `scale_y`, centered inside the frame. This prevents the circular clock from becoming narrow or wide.
- Recompute that proportional geometry whenever Shell reallocates the frame during the window-picker and Show Applications transition.
- Preserve v1.3.3's compact Show Applications button in the custom left dock, hidden GNOME bottom dash, market behavior, and all other accepted desktop features.

## Diagnosis

`Clutter.Clone` normally scales its source independently in the horizontal and
vertical directions to fill whatever allocation it receives. Even inside the
correct wallpaper group, a transitional allocation with a slightly different
shape can therefore squeeze the clock. The v1.3.4 frame leaves the clone's
native allocation unchanged and uses a single actor transform for both axes.
