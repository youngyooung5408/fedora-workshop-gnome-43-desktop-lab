## Diagnosis

v1.2.15 did not intentionally change the dock, clock, stock layout, GNOME
settings, or extension stylesheet. Those presentation inputs are identical to
v1.2.14. The visual regression happened because the new focus-window startup
check immediately called `set_visible()` on the dock reveal `St.Widget`. GNOME
Shell 49 does not expose that method on this actor, so extension startup threw a
`TypeError` and stopped after creating only part of the desktop UI. GNOME Shell
then reported `desktop-lab-v12@young` in the `ERROR` state and unloaded the
extension stylesheet without calling `disable()` to remove the actors already
created. The surviving unstyled dock, clock, and market actors therefore used
markedly different default sizing and formatting.

The same invalid call existed in v1.2.14, but it was normally reached only after
a fullscreen-state change. v1.2.15 made the latent error deterministic by
running the suppression synchronization during `enable()`.

## Repair

- Keep all v1.2.15 active-window suppression signals and geometry policy.
